Yow-Ting Shiue [ full CV ]

My name is Yow-Ting Shiue (薛祐婷). I am currently a research assistant in the Natural Language Processing Lab, National Taiwan University. I have research experience in both Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Information Retrieval (IR). I conducted or participated in projects such as Chinese writing error detection and correction, incorporating lexical knowledge into word and sense embeddings, understanding crowd financial opinion, and open-domain concept attribute extraction from text. My broader research goal is to enable communication between human intelligence and machine intelligence in the form of natural languages, in which the subtlety of a variety of knowledge can be described. With the realization of this goal, people and computers can learn and grow together.
